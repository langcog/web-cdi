Web-CDI is sponsored by the MacArthur-Bates Communicative Development Inventory (CDI) Advisory Board. The English long forms (American/Canadian English WG and WS), Mexican Spanish long forms (WG and WS), English CDI III, and English/Mexican Spanish CDI-CAT are distributed through Brookes Publishing Company and require an access code that can be purchased at https://brookespublishing.com/products/cdi. More information about the English CDI suite of instruments can be found in the MacArthur-Bates User's Guide and Technical Manual, 3rd Edition (Marchman, Dale, and Fenson, 2023), also available from Brookes Publishing Company. See deMayo et al. (2021) Language Development Research for more information about Web-CDI; See Kachergis et al. (2022) Journal of Speech, Language & Hearing Research for more information about CDI-CAT.

If you're a parent, please follow the link sent to you by a researcher to fill out an MB-CDI form. If you lost your link, please contact your researcher or the Web-CDI team.
